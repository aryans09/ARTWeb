Priyanka Kukreja CEO, Google

Currently the Dean, Graduate Studies at Cleveland State University. His research interests are in the areas of Computer Science education and the Internet of Things. His work is funded by grants from the National Science Foundation and the Cleveland Foundation.

Nigamnath Shridhar Dean,Cleveland State University

Ph.D. Candidate at UCL, ex-{Microsoft Research, Bing, Goldman Sachs}. He is interested in teaching machines to better understand (& thereby model) users & their needs. His research interests lie at the intersection of Machine Learning, User Modelling & Personalization. His current Ph.D. research focuses on developing efficient machine learning models for user tasks & need understanding, knowledge discovery and decision optimization. He also collaborates on research related to Web Search & Information Retrieval.
